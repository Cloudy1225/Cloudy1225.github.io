I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Software Institute, Nanjing University. My research interests revolve around graph machine learning, such as self-supervised learning, deep graph clustering, and scalable graph learning. I am also intrigued by the downstream applications of graph machine learning, including fraud detection and recommender systems. Furthermore, I have a keen interest in exploring the intersection of graphs with large language models and biomedical sciences.
